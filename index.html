About Us

VSV Enterprises Private Limited specializes in all kinds of industrial material handling, transportation, manpower supply and yard management activities. VSV Enterprises has an excellent track record with our prime clients like Tata Steel Ltd, Steel Authority of India Ltd, Indian Oil Corporation Ltd, Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Ltd, Jharkhand Health Medical Education & Family Welfare Department, Jharkhand Secondary Education Project Council, etc. The company was founded in 2007 and since then has set the benchmark for the highest quality and safety standards in the steel, iron, logistics and construction sectors. The company currently has operations in multiple locations in Jharkhand and Odisha.

Service Portfolio

Equipment Shifting

We use low-bed and other specialized trailers to shift large segments within a plant's premises.

Raw material handling

We offer complete solutions from loading, transportation to unloading of industrial raw materials such as lime, sinter, iron ore, etc. We also offer the same services for industrial byproducts such as BF Slag, LD slag and aggregates.

Rake loading

We offer complete solutions for rake loading which comprises of yard management, liaising with Indian Railways and security

Yard management

We manage all kinds of yards by providing security, housekeeping and safety services, which allows us to create a safe, clean and organized working environment while also enabling smoother inward and outward movement of materials.

Scrap collection

We use a diverse range of equipment such as flatbed trailers, low-bed trailers, tippers and dumper placers to collect and transport various shapes and sizes of ferrous and nonferrous scrap.

Scrap handling

Ferrous and nonferrous scrap is unloaded at the yard by Liebherr mobile magnet cranes which also come with orange peel grab attachment. These machines are tailor-made for scrap handling activities and offer the best-in-class efficiency, operator comfort and safety

Shipping management

We provide road and railway freight shipping services which comprise of stockyard management, outward and inward stock movement administration, data recording, compilation and management by providing SAP terminal operators and stock management software operators

Manpower supply

Our manpower supply services offer highly skilled, skilled and semi-skilled manpower for a plethora of jobs that range from manual labour for scrap segregation, drivers for HMV to operators for specialized equipment such as overhead cranes.
